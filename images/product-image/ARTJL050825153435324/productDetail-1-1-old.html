🧾 Product Description

CHICCO FEEDING AND SOOTHING BIBS are specially designed for infants aged 0 months and above to help keep them clean during feeding and soothing. These bibs are made of soft and absorbent materials to protect your baby's clothes from spills and drools while providing comfort. The adjustable closure ensures a secure fit and easy wear. The bibs come in a variety of cute designs and colors to make feeding time fun and stylish. Chicco is a well-known brand that offers a wide range of baby care products designed to promote the health and well-being of infants and young children.

🍼 Uses

  • To protect the baby's clothing during feeding and soothing
  • To keep the baby clean and dry during meals and teething

👶 Side Effects

  • No known side effects when using the product as intended.

📦 How To Use

Simply place the bib around your baby's neck with the adjustable closure, making sure it covers the front adequately to catch spills and drools.

🧺 Storage

  • Store the bibs in a clean and dry place when not in use.
  • Wash the bibs as per the care instructions provided to maintain hygiene.
