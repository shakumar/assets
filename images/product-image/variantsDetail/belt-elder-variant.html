Measuring your belt size

Measure a belt you currently wear (in inches) from the point where the buckle attaches to the belt to the belt hole you are currently using. If this measurement is 34 inches, order a size 34 belt.
The size-34 belt that you order will be 34 inches from the point where the buckle attaches to the belt to the middle belt hole.
IMPORTANT:Do not measure the belt from end to end. You will not get the accurate size that way.
