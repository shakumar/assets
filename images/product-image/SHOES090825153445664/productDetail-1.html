Design Concept

Sports Fusion Fashion — designed with advanced technology in the upper and sole materials. Featuring skin-friendly Suede and TPR outsole, crafted with precision.

Material

Skin-friendly Suede ensures breathability, comfort, and lightweight performance. Rapid sweat absorption keeps your feet cool and dry all day long.

Sole

Durable rubber soles offer ultra-light cushioning with flexibility from vertical and horizontal flex grooves—perfect for every movement.

Characteristics

These sneakers are equipped with antimicrobial linings to fight odor-causing bacteria—keeping your feet fresh all day.
